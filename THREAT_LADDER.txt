LEVEL 0 — OBSERVE
- small wobble
- no immediate disruption
- continue monitoring

LEVEL 1 — REINFORCE
- mild deviation
- remind staff of safe procedures
- increase visibility

LEVEL 2 — STABILIZE
- moderate disruption
- restrict risky actions
- preserve essential services
- prepare fallback

LEVEL 3 — ISOLATE
- serious threat
- segment affected systems
- contain spread
- lock high-risk pathways

LEVEL 4 — RESTORE
- confirmed damaging event
- recover from trusted state
- verify integrity before re-entry

LEVEL 5 — ESCALATE
- human leadership, security, and emergency response engaged
- external coordination if required
