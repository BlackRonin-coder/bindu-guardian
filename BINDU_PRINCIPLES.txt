1. Protect people first, then processes, then devices.
2. Detect deviation, do not panic at noise.
3. Interpret proportionately, not rigidly.
4. Use layered response: observe, reinforce, stabilize, isolate, restore, escalate.
5. Preserve human override and manual fallback.
6. Log decisions and actions for auditability.
7. Reject uncontrolled autonomy.
8. Build for hospitals, universities, and agencies as real environments.
