# Bindu Guardian Use Cases

## Hospital

Potential use:
- recognise instability across access systems, communications, and critical workflows
- recommend selective containment rather than blunt shutdown
- preserve patient care and medication continuity where safely possible

## University

Potential use:
- identify coordinated instability affecting identity, research data, and teaching continuity
- distinguish local disruption from broader threat
- support proportionate response without unnecessary interruption

## Public Agency

Potential use:
- recognise threats affecting identity, records, communications, and service continuity
- preserve essential public function
- support auditable defensive decision-making

## General high-risk systems

Bindu Guardian is relevant where:
- safety matters
- continuity matters
- opaque decisions create risk
- overreaction can cause secondary harm
